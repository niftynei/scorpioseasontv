Welcome to Scorpio Season! A show devoted to reviewing the State of the Universe in alphabetical order, hosted by Lisa Neigut and Venkatesh Rao. Each season is 26 episodes (A-Z), and each episode explores topics under the letter of the week (LOTW).

You can subscribe on YouTube for the full video experience, or via Apple Podcasts, Google Podcasts, or Spotify for the audio-only experience.

Follow us on Twitter @scorpioseasontv for updates, show notes, etc.

Season 2: Adventures in Hapless Hackery

Premiering on October 23, 2020! Season 2 will review the State of the Universe in alphabetical order from the perspective of DIY, hacking, making, repairs, junk, building, and tools. As they say, when you have a hammer in your hand, everything looks like a nail. We'll be trying to look at the universe as a bunch of nails. If you're making something cool you'd like to show off on Scorpio Season, get in touch.
